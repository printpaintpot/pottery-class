About Us

Print Paint Pot - Pottery Class Sydney

Print Paint Pot is a small family-owned independent pottery business with classes listed on ClassBento’s online marketplace. We are a team of passionate pottery teachers thrilled to share the joy of clay with a variety of pottery classes at our cosy pottery studio. Our website utilises ClassBento’s booking system incurring a 5% commission instead of 20% when booking via their online marketplace. Thank you for booking via our official website, it means the world to our small dedicated team at Print Paint Pot
